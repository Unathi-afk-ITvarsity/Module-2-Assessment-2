About

Welcome to Pick N Pay, your trusted partner in everyday shopping. Established in 1967 in South Africa, Pick N Pay has grown into one of the leading retail chains in the country, renowned for our commitment to providing quality products, exceptional value, and outstanding customer service.

Our mission is to offer an exceptional shopping experience by delivering a wide range of high-quality products at affordable prices. At Pick N Pay, we are dedicated to meeting the needs of our diverse customer base, ensuring that every visit to our stores is convenient, enjoyable, and rewarding. We believe in building long-lasting relationships with our customers through trust, reliability, and excellence.

Sustainability is a core value at Pick N Pay. We are committed to reducing our environmental impact through various initiatives, including waste reduction, energy efficiency, and sustainable sourcing practices. Our goal is to contribute positively to our communities and the environment, while offering products that support a healthier and more sustainable lifestyle.

Innovation and community engagement are at the heart of what we do. We continuously seek ways to enhance our offerings and services, leveraging the latest technology and trends to provide a modern shopping experience. Our dedication to supporting local communities is reflected in our efforts to promote local suppliers and contribute to various social initiatives.

As we continue to evolve, our commitment to our core values of quality, value, and customer satisfaction remains unwavering. Thank you for choosing Pick N Pay for your shopping needs and for being a part of our journey. We look forward to serving you and your family with the best products and services.
