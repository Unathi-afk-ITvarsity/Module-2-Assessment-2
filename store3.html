About

Welcome to Adidas, a global leader in sportswear and athletic innovation. Founded in 1949 in Herzogenaurach, Germany, Adidas has grown into one of the world’s most iconic sports brands, renowned for its commitment to performance, quality, and style.

Our mission is to be the best sports brand in the world. We aim to inspire and support athletes of all levels by providing high-performance products that enhance their experience and help them achieve their goals. At Adidas, we believe in the power of sport to change lives and are dedicated to creating products that reflect our passion for excellence and innovation.

Sustainability is a core focus for us. We are committed to reducing our environmental impact through initiatives such as using recycled materials, minimizing waste, and adopting sustainable production practices. Our goal is to contribute positively to the environment and communities while delivering the best possible products to our customers.

Innovation is at the heart of everything we do. From pioneering advancements in footwear technology to collaborating with leading designers and athletes, Adidas is constantly pushing the boundaries of performance and style. Our cutting-edge research and development ensure that we deliver products that meet the highest standards of quality and functionality.

As we continue to grow and evolve, our commitment to our core values—performance, innovation, and sustainability—remains steadfast. We are proud of our heritage and excited about the future, working tirelessly to inspire and support athletes around the globe.
